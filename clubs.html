Society of Women Engineers (SWE)

For the past two years, I have served as a High School Girls Day Counselor with the Society of Women Engineers (SWE) at the University of Oklahoma. As a counselor, I guided students through engaging activities, answered questions about college and STEM pathways, and helped foster a supportive environment where young girls could see themselves as future engineers.
