Internship Experience

During the summer of 2024, I was blessed with the opportunity to intern with the Houston Astros Foundation for their Summer Robotics Camp sponsored by Honeywell. I joined this initiative through Kids Xplorer, a nonprofit dedicated to expanding STEM access across Houston. Through this internship, I had the privilege of working directly with the Founder and CEO, Mrs. Sonia Jordan, whose leadership, vision, and dedication to community uplift left a lasting impact on me.

Each week, I collaborated with a team of interns and led groups of 1st through 8th grade students from neighborhoods across the Houston area. Our focus was teaching them how to build, assemble, and code VEX IQ robots, giving these students a hands-on introduction to robotics and computer science. At the end of every week, these young innovators proudly showcased their robots during Sunday games at Daikin Park, where their families joined in cheering them on at a Major League stadium.

Beyond robotics, I was responsible for leading multiple creative STEM activities. Some of these included:

  • Designing and assembling working electric fan models
  • Teaching kids to build pocket flashlights using foam, stickers, and batteries
  • Introducing binary code and its real-world tech applications
  • Constructing working paper circuits using copper tape and LED lights
